Meet the Owner

Robert Johnson,
Master Electrician

Robert Johnson founded Johnson's Electric of Charlotte in 2003 after 6 years as a journeyman electrician with two of the region's largest electrical contractors. What started as a one-man operation with a used work van has grown into a 12-person team serving the entire Charlotte metro area.

Robert holds a North Carolina Master Electrician license and is licensed in South Carolina — one of only a small percentage of electricians in the region to hold dual-state master credentials. His hands-on approach means he's never more than a phone call away, and he personally reviews every major project before it goes live.

"My dad was an electrician. I grew up respecting the work — how critical it is to get it exactly right, every time. Electricity doesn't forgive shortcuts, and neither do I." — Robert Johnson

Our Mission

Guiding Principles of Everything We Do

Our values aren't a slogan — they're the standards we hold ourselves to on every single job, every single day.

Safety First, Always

Electrical work carries real risk. Every technician on our team is OSHA-certified and trained in the latest NEC code requirements. We never cut corners — your family's safety and your property are always our top priority.

Uncompromising Quality

We use only manufacturer-recommended materials and follow every local and national code to the letter. Our work is inspected, documented, and backed by a written warranty — because quality you can't see should be just as solid as quality you can.

Community Commitment

We're Charlotte natives who live and work in the communities we serve. We sponsor local youth sports leagues, support Charlotte's small businesses, and hire locally. When you hire us, your money stays in the community.

Honest & Transparent

Upfront pricing with no surprise fees. We explain every recommendation, every option, and every line item before we start work. Our customers know exactly what they're paying for — and why.

Responsive & Reliable

We answer our phones. We show up on time. We call if we're running late. It sounds simple, but we know from our customers that reliable communication is what truly sets a great contractor apart from an average one.

Continuous Education

Electrical codes evolve, technology advances, and we keep pace. Every technician completes ongoing training through NFPA, OSHA, and manufacturer certification programs — so you always get the most current, safest solutions.

Our History

Two Decades of Growth & Service

From a one-man startup to Charlotte's go-to electrical contractor — here's how we got here.

2003

Founded by Robert Johnson

Robert Johnson registered Johnson's Electric of Charlotte as a sole proprietorship. With 6 years of journeyman experience and a Master Electrician license in hand, he started with residential service calls across south Charlotte.

2006

First Commercial Contracts

Expanded services to light commercial clients. Signed first contracts with local retail developers in Ballantyne, growing the team to 3 licensed electricians and a dedicated office coordinator.

2010

BBB Accreditation & SC Expansion

Achieved Better Business Bureau (BBB) accreditation and expanded service territory into York County, South Carolina. Obtained dual-state licensure in North and South Carolina, a distinction held by only a handful of Charlotte-area contractors.

2014

OSHA & NFPA Certification Program

Launched a company-wide safety certification program. All field technicians completed OSHA 10-Hour training; senior staff achieved OSHA 30-Hour status. Partnered with NFPA for ongoing NEC code compliance education.

2018

EV Charging & Smart Home Division

Recognized the surge in electric vehicles and smart home technology. Became an authorized installer for Level 2 EV charging stations and launched a dedicated smart home electrical division serving the growing Charlotte-area EV market.

2021

Generator Division & 24/7 Emergency Launch

After back-to-back storm seasons highlighted the need for whole-home backup power, we formalized our generator installation and service division. Launched official 24/7 emergency response service covering the full Charlotte metro area.

Today

12 Electricians, 30+ Cities, Still Growing

Johnson's Electric now employs 12 full-time licensed electricians, serves 30+ cities across the Charlotte metro, and completes over 500 residential and commercial projects each year. We're proud to be the electrical contractor Charlotte trusts most — and we're just getting started.
