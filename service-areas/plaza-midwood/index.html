Common Questions

FAQs for Plaza Midwood Residents

Answers to common electrical questions from our customers in the Plaza Midwood area.

My Plaza Midwood home still has knob-and-tube wiring. Is that a problem?

Yes — knob-and-tube wiring lacks a ground wire, cannot support today's electrical loads, and is a fire hazard. Most insurers won't cover homes with active K&T wiring. We specialize in safely replacing it with modern wiring while minimizing damage to your walls.

Can you upgrade my old 100-amp panel to 200 amps?

Absolutely. Most older Plaza Midwood homes have 100-amp service, which isn't enough for modern appliances and electronics. We handle the full panel upgrade including coordination with Duke Energy for the service upgrade.

Do you work in the restaurants and bars on Central Avenue?

Yes. We're very familiar with the electrical demands of Central Avenue's restaurant and bar scene — commercial kitchen circuits, hood ventilation wiring, bar lighting, and compliance with Charlotte's commercial electrical codes.

Will you have to tear up my walls to rewire my bungalow?

We use low-invasive rewiring techniques wherever possible to preserve the historic character of Plaza Midwood bungalows. We'll discuss the approach and expected scope before any work begins.
