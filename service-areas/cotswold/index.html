My Cotswold home was built in the 1960s — should I be concerned about the wiring?

Homes from this era sometimes have aluminum wiring or undersized panels that can be fire hazards. We offer a free wiring inspection to assess your Cotswold home's electrical system and give you an honest recommendation.

Can you upgrade my panel without major wall damage?

In most cases, yes. Panel upgrades primarily involve work at the panel location and the service entry — we protect floors and walls and leave your home as clean as we found it.

Do you handle electrical for Cotswold kitchen remodels?

Absolutely. Kitchen remodels often require new circuits for appliances, island outlets, under-cabinet lighting, and exhaust fans. We work directly with your contractor and pull all necessary permits.

Are you familiar with the Cotswold and Sardis Road commercial area?

Yes. We service retail spaces, restaurants, and professional offices throughout the Cotswold commercial corridor and can handle everything from TI electrical work to exterior signage wiring.
