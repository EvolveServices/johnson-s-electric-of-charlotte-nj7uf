Can you install a Level 2 EV charger in my Ballantyne garage?

Yes. This is one of the most common requests we get in Ballantyne. We install all major Level 2 home chargers, run the dedicated 240V circuit, and handle the permit with Mecklenburg County — typically completed in one visit.

Do you connect standby generators to homes in Ballantyne?

Absolutely. We install the transfer switch, run the electrical connection to your Generac or other standby generator, and test the system to ensure seamless automatic operation during a Duke Energy outage.

My Ballantyne home has a 200-amp panel — is that enough for two EVs and a pool?

It depends on your overall load. With two EV chargers, a pool pump, and a large HVAC system, you may need a 320A or 400A panel upgrade or a load management system. We'll perform a load calculation and give you the most cost-effective solution.
