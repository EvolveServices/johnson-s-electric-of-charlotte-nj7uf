Journeyman Electrician

Full-Time Charlotte, NC Residential + Commercial

We're looking for a licensed Journeyman Electrician to join our growing team. You'll work on a variety of residential and light commercial projects throughout the Charlotte metro, from panel upgrades and rewires to EV charger installs and service calls. Apprentice Electrician

Full-Time Charlotte, NC Entry Level

Starting your electrical career? We want to hear from you. Our apprentice program pairs you with experienced journeyman and master electricians who are genuinely committed to teaching the trade. You'll get hands-on training on real jobs, not just grunt work, and we'll support you through your apprenticeship program hours and classroom requirements.

We pay for your apprenticeship program enrollment and give you scheduled raises as you progress through each year of your apprenticeship. Your future is worth investing in.

Requirements

  • High school diploma or GED
  • Enrolled in or willing to enroll in a NCCER apprenticeship
  • Reliable transportation and a valid driver's license
  • Willingness to learn and a positive attitude
  • Ability to pass a background check and drug screen

Our Culture

A Team Built on Respect and Results

We believe great work comes from people who feel valued. Our culture reflects what we expect on every job site.

Safety First, Always

We don't cut corners on safety. Ever. Our team follows strict safety protocols on every job, and we empower every technician to stop a job if something doesn't look right. No deadline is worth an injury.

Respect on the Job Site

We treat every team member with respect regardless of their position or experience level. Veterans mentor apprentices, not bark at them. A good team elevates everyone.

Quality You Can Be Proud Of

We take pride in doing the job right the first time. Our standards are high because our customers depend on us, and we want every technician to leave a job site knowing they did their best work.

Growth is Encouraged

Whether you want to move from apprentice to journeyman or from journeyman to master, we actively support your advancement. Promotions come from within. Hard work gets noticed here.

Integrity in Everything

We deal honestly with our customers and with each other. No upselling, no shortcuts, no excuses. We do what we say we're going to do, and we expect the same from our team.

Rooted in Charlotte

We're a locally owned company that serves our neighbors. Our team lives and works in the Charlotte area, and we're committed to being a positive part of this community for the long term.
