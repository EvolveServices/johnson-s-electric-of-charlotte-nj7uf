Johnson's Electric of Charlotte ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website at johnsonselectriccharlotte.com or contact us for electrical services. Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the site.

1 Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us as well as information gathered automatically through your use of the website.

Information you provide directly:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and service address when you submit a contact or quote request form
  • Details about your electrical project or service needs that you include in your message
  • Payment information when processed for services rendered (handled securely through our payment processor)
  • Any other information you voluntarily provide when communicating with us by phone, email, or in person

Information collected automatically:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type and version, operating system, and device type
  • Pages visited, time spent on pages, and referring URLs
  • Date and time of your visit
  • Clickstream data and site interaction patterns

2 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Responding to your service inquiries, quote requests, and contact form submissions
  • Scheduling electrical service appointments and coordinating with our technicians
  • Processing payments and sending invoices or receipts
  • Sending confirmation emails, appointment reminders, and follow-up communications
  • Improving our website content, layout, and user experience based on usage analytics
  • Sending occasional service updates or promotional communications (you may opt out at any time)
  • Complying with applicable laws, regulations, and legal obligations
  • Protecting against fraudulent, abusive, or unlawful use of our services

We will not use your information for any purpose incompatible with the purposes described above without first obtaining your consent.

3 Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and gather analytics data. A cookie is a small text file placed on your device when you visit a website.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential cookies: Required for basic site functionality such as form submission and navigation. These cannot be disabled without affecting site performance.
  • Analytics cookies: Used to understand how visitors interact with our website (pages visited, session duration, traffic sources). We use this data in aggregate form to improve our content.
  • Marketing cookies: May be set by third-party advertising platforms (such as Google Ads) to show you relevant advertisements on other websites you visit.
  • Preference cookies: Remember settings or choices you make to improve your return visits.

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies, delete existing cookies, or receive a notification when cookies are being set. Note that disabling certain cookies may limit the functionality of our website.

We do not use cookies to collect sensitive personal information or to track your activity across unaffiliated websites except as permitted by applicable law.

4 Third-Party Services

We use reputable third-party service providers to help operate our business and website. These providers may have access to certain information only to the extent needed to perform their specific functions:

  • Google Analytics: Website traffic and behavior analytics. Google's data practices are governed by the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Ads / Google Tag Manager: Advertising and conversion tracking to measure the effectiveness of our online marketing campaigns.
  • Email service providers: Used to send transactional emails such as contact form confirmations and appointment reminders.
  • Payment processors: Secure processing of any payments related to services. We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers.
  • Scheduling software: Appointment booking and dispatch management tools used by our office team.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their own marketing purposes. Any third-party providers we engage are required to maintain the confidentiality and security of your information and are prohibited from using it for any purpose other than the services they provide to us.

5 Information Sharing & Disclosure

We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service delivery: With our field technicians and office staff to fulfill your service request or appointment.
  • Legal compliance: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect the rights and safety of our company, employees, or the public.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our business assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you before such a transfer takes effect.
  • With your consent: In any other circumstances, only with your prior written or electronic consent.

We do not share your personal information with unaffiliated third parties for their own marketing or commercial purposes.

6 Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously and implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmitted through our website
  • Restricted access to personal information on a need-to-know basis among our staff
  • Regular review of our data collection, storage, and processing practices
  • Secure, encrypted storage of any retained customer records

Despite our best efforts, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you believe your information has been compromised, please contact us immediately at privacy@johnsonselectriccharlotte.com.

7 Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information. These may include:

  • Access: The right to request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: The right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: The right to request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Opt-out of marketing: The right to unsubscribe from our marketing emails at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in any email or by contacting us directly.
  • Data portability: The right to request your data in a portable, machine-readable format where technically feasible.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information in Section 10 below. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe and in accordance with applicable law. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.
